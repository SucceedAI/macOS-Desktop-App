Succeed AI turns selected text or a short /ai command into finished writing directly inside Mail, Notes, browsers, documents, and other editable Mac fields. Select text and choose a one-tap outcome from the menu bar, or type an instruction, optionally paste the source, and press Return. SucceedAI replaces only the exact, unchanged source in place.

No chatbot tab. No copy-and-paste loop. No account, subscription, API key, or server. Apple’s on-device language model performs the work locally, even offline.

Use SucceedAI to:

- Rewrite emails so they are clearer, warmer, shorter, or more confident.
- Summarize long notes without losing key facts or commitments.
- Extract action items with owners, dates, and dependencies kept intact.
- Turn rough goals and constraints into an ordered plan with clear next steps.
- Translate messages without opening another app.
- Paste an email, notes, or multiline source after your instruction and transform it in place.
- Draft customer support replies inside your browser or help desk.
- Polish product copy, social posts, issue comments, release notes, and documentation.
- Turn rough thoughts into finished writing without breaking focus.
- Select existing text and choose Polish, Shorten, Draft Reply, Summarize, Action Items, Make a Plan, or Translate without writing a prompt.

Private by design:

- Lives quietly in the menu bar.
- Works in editable Mac text fields.
- Runs with Apple’s on-device language model.
- Keeps prompts and responses on your Mac.
- Works without an account, API key, subscription, or backend server.
- Includes a guided Input Monitoring and Accessibility permission setup.
- Lets you customize the replacement trigger from Settings.
- Includes a quick composer for writing directly from the menu bar.
- Shows every selected-text outcome at once in a compact one-tap action grid.
- Includes non-destructive Polish, Shorten, Summarize, Draft Reply, Action Items, Make a Plan, and target-language Translate workflows.
- Lets you stop generation or refine a completed result into the next draft.
- Adds eight dedicated Shortcuts actions that automatically accept the previous action’s output for private local automations.
- Shows a clear hourglass while a local response is being written.
- Reads the current editable field only after you invoke the trigger so pasted text is included accurately.
- Re-checks the app, field, exact command or selection, cursor, and document fingerprint before replacing anything.
- Cancels immediately and leaves the original command untouched if you switch apps, click, continue typing, stop, or generation cannot finish.
- Coordinates local requests and briefly retries when Apple’s model is busy.

Succeed AI is designed for founders, operators, writers, developers, support teams, marketers, and anyone who writes across multiple Mac apps every day.

The goal is simple: private AI writing should not force you to leave the app you are already using.

Requires macOS 26, Apple silicon, and Apple Intelligence enabled.
