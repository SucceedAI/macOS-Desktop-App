# Launch Checklist

## Before Launch

- Confirm production API key is configured in local release build.
- Confirm Accessibility permission flow is clear on a fresh macOS install.
- Confirm Settings > Keys can change and restore the replacement trigger.
- Confirm replacement works in Apple Notes, Mail, Safari/Chrome text fields, and at least one messaging app.
- Confirm App Store screenshots are `2880x1800` PNG files.
- Confirm Product Hunt gallery images are `1270x760` and under file limits.
- Upload Product Hunt icon from `ProductHunt/icons/product-hunt-icon-240x240.png`.
- Use the first gallery image as the hook; keep it first.
- Prepare the maker first comment before launch.
- Prepare support replies for Accessibility, privacy, app compatibility, pricing, and roadmap.
- Prepare social posts and outreach messages.
- Prepare a short demo video if possible. Product Hunt supports YouTube links for video galleries.

## Launch Day

- Publish at the start of the Product Hunt day if possible.
- Post the maker first comment immediately.
- Reply to every useful comment with specific detail.
- Share on personal X, LinkedIn, Bluesky, relevant communities, and newsletter contacts.
- Ask for feedback, not upvotes.
- Track objections and update FAQ copy.
- Screenshot early positive comments for later social proof.
- Monitor crash reports, API errors, support emails, and permission confusion.

## First 48 Hours After Launch

- Add common Product Hunt questions to the landing page FAQ.
- Turn strong comments into testimonials if permission is granted.
- Create a short blog post: `How SucceedAI removes the copy-paste loop from AI writing on Mac`.
- Send follow-up to creators who replied.
- Update App Store description with language that resonated during launch.
- Identify the top three workflows users asked for and turn them into roadmap issues.

## First 30 Days

- Publish 4 SEO posts:
  1. AI writing assistant for Mac: how in-place text replacement works.
  2. How to use AI in any Mac app without copy-paste.
  3. Why SucceedAI needs Accessibility permission on macOS.
  4. Best AI writing workflows for Mail, Notes, and browser text fields.
- Add comparison pages only when they are fair, accurate, and useful.
- Build a lightweight changelog page.
- Add customer quotes to the homepage.
- Create a 30-second demo video and reuse it in Product Hunt, App Store, and social posts.
