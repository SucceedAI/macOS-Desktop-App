# Outreach Sequences

## Rules

- Do not ask people to upvote. Ask for feedback or a comment if they find it useful.
- Personalize the first line for every recipient.
- Send to people who already care about Mac productivity, writing tools, AI workflows, or indie software.
- Keep each message short enough to read on mobile.

## Audience Segments

1. Mac productivity creators.
2. AI workflow newsletter writers.
3. Indie app reviewers.
4. Founders who post about productivity.
5. Support and customer success leaders.
6. Technical writers and developer advocates.
7. Solopreneurs and consultants.
