# Brand Guide

## Brand Name

SucceedAI

Use `SucceedAI` as one word in product copy, filenames, and listing metadata.

## Category

AI text replacement for macOS.

Secondary category language:
- AI writing assistant for Mac.
- Menu bar AI writing assistant.
- AI productivity app for macOS.

## Positioning Statement

SucceedAI helps Mac users write with AI inside the app they are already using by replacing typed commands with generated text in place.

## Core Message

Write with AI in any Mac app without switching context.

## Supporting Messages

- Replace rough prompts with polished text in place.
- Remove the copy-paste loop from AI writing.
- Use AI inside Mail, Notes, browsers, messaging apps, and documents.
- Customize the command trigger from Settings > Keys.
- Understand macOS Accessibility permission before enabling it.

## Voice

SucceedAI should sound:
- Clear.
- Native to Mac users.
- Fast and practical.
- Calm about permissions.
- Specific about workflows.

Avoid sounding:
- Hype-heavy.
- Vague about privacy or permissions.
- Overbroad about working perfectly everywhere.
- Like a generic AI chatbot.

## Color Direction

Primary:
- Deep ink: `#0b1e25`
- Teal: `#12bfb2`
- Product Hunt orange: `#ff6135`

Secondary:
- Blue: `#2276ff`
- Warm cream: `#faf7ee`
- Soft mint: `#e6f8f3`

## Image Style

- Use real Mac UI screenshots inside soft window frames.
- Keep headlines short and benefit-led.
- Prefer light, calm gradients for Product Hunt gallery images.
- Use dark, high-contrast gradients for campaign covers.
- Keep the first Product Hunt image centered because it may be cropped into thumbnails and social cards.

## Approved One-Liners

- AI text replacement in any Mac app.
- Write with AI in any Mac app.
- Use AI on Mac without copy-paste.
- AI writing without context switching.
- Replace rough prompts with polished text.

## Avoid

- The best AI writing app.
- Works perfectly in every app.
- Private by default unless the implemented network and data policy supports that claim.
- Never leaves your device if API calls are required.
- Guaranteed productivity results.
